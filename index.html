DEPLOYED ASSETS

Cycle 25-26

Velmora: The Fallen Kingdom

A three-stage action game set in a corrupted realm on the brink of collapse. A nameless warrior awakens with one purpose and that is to fight through the darkness that consumed Velmora and seal the Gate of Ruin before nothing is left to save.

Each island presents a distinct challenge: survive relentless zombie waves in Corpse Hollow, protect the villagers of DragonBane from a vicious dragon, and face the Demon King himself. Three islands. Three battles. One chance.

Developed by: Shraddha Pant (ECE-AI), Neha (CSE-AI), Samriddhi (ECE-AI), Zainab Hussain (MAE)
Link

Serpent's Second Chance

A reimagined Snake and Ladder in the Kingdom of Ascension. Landing on a serpent triggers a mini-game challenge — Guess the Card, Hangman, or Avoid Falling Objects. Prove your skill and continue unharmed. Fail, and you slide back.

Climb the ladders, outsmart the serpents, and fight your way to Tile 100.

Developed by: Ojaswi (CSE), Palak Jain (CSE), Vatika (CSE-AI), Drishti (CSE)
Link

Zero Hour: Escape

A psychological escape room where you play as Subject 027, placed inside a controlled cognitive experiment disguised as a cozy, ordinary room. Locked doors, a ticking clock, and intentionally placed objects slowly reveal the truth.

Navigate slider puzzles, safes, chess logic, card patterns and locks. Escape, and you're fit for deployment. Fail, and the trial concludes without you.

Developed by: Zainab Tanweer (ECE), Priyanshi Jaiswal (MAC), Astha Singh (MAE), Dishita Gupta (CSE)
Link

DEPLOYED ASSETS

Cycle 24-25

Astro Drifting

A space racing game where you pilot a rocket through a cosmic obstacle course, collecting golden coins, grabbing fuel relics to stay alive, and dodging Time-Turtles that slow you down. Set in the lore of the lost Ancients of Orbiton.

Survive the void, outlast the chaos, and drift your way into legend.

Developed by: Kavya Chaudhari (CSE), Palak Bhatia (AIML), Lavanaya Khosla (CSE), Aksa (CSE), Anisha Gupta (CSE-AI), Navya Gupta (ECE), Priya Kumari (CSE), Priyanshi (ECE-AI), Trisha Tomy (IT), Yashika Ashra (MAE)
Link

Pentara

A combat game rooted in betrayal, legacy, and the fight to keep a forbidden power buried. Five warriors trained under the same master, each carrying a fragment of the legendary Hidden Scroll. Two turn against the pact — the remaining three must fight back.

Face off against Samurai opponents where every battle carries the weight of a centuries-old secret.

Developed by: Mahak (CSE-AI), Khushi (ECE-AI), Vanisha (CSE), Kritika (CSE), Kirti (CSE), Ekamjot (CSE)
Link

Blingo

A digital reimagining of classic Bingo as a 6x8 Player vs AI battle. Match the called numbers, complete a row, and win before the AI does. No strategy, no skill gap — just you, a bot that takes Bingo very seriously, and the pure chaos of chance.

Developed by: Vansika Rana (CSE), Jyoti Choudhary (CSE-AI)
Link

Fred in the Fright House

A 3D horror-puzzle adventure where you guide Fred (a self-declared coward) through a haunted mansion. Dared by his friends, Fred steps inside and immediately regrets everything. Team up with a friendly spirit to find 3 hidden keys while dodging every ghost.

Sneak through creepy hallways, crack candle puzzles, color codes, and symbol locks. Scary? Yes. Fun? Absolutely.

Developed by: Akanksha Bathla (CSE), Harshita Agarwal (CSE-AI), Prachi Kumari (CSE-AI), Swarnima (CSE), Vanshika (CSE-AI), Vedika (CSE-AI)
Link
